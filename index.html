Welcome To JCM Group Of Schools

Welcome to JCM Group of Schools, where we are committed to providing a nurturing and enriching educational environment. Our first branch, located in Mbundane, opened its doors in January 2025, offering classes from ECD to Grade 5. We're excited to announce that we are now enrolling students up to Grade 6 for the upcoming year! Beyond a solid academic foundation, students enjoy a variety of activities including swimming, chess, and foreign language instruction.

We are also opening JCM Academy in Esigodini next year (January 2026)! This new branch will eventually offer education from ECD up to Form 4. However, for the initial intake, we are actively enrolling students from ECD to Grade 6. Join the JCM family and secure a bright future for your child.
